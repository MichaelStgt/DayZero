Project Requirements Specification

Phase 1: App Identity & Core Objectives

  • App Name: DayZero
  • Primary Goal: Provide a discrete, highly effective tool to help users initiate and maintain sobriety from alcohol and nicotine.
  • Target Audience: Individuals at any stage of recovery (Day 1 through long-term maintenance).

1.1 Naming Rationale & Decision

When brainstorming app names, a major factor considered was discretion. Users often prefer an app name and icon that do not immediately signal "addiction recovery" to bystanders. The explored directions included:

  • Focus on the Journey: Names like Pivot and Momentum embrace the process of change.
  • Focus on the Outcome: Names like Clarity and Horizon highlight what the user is gaining, keeping the app discreet.
  • Action-Oriented: Short, punchy names like Nix and Detach sound modern and carry relevant meaning.

Final Decision: DayZero

Reasoning: While "Nix" is a strong action word, it translates to "nothing" in German slang, which is unsuitable for the target market in the German App Store. "DayZero" was selected because it effectively reframes the starting point of sobriety as a powerful foundation rather than a deficit, applying equally well to brand-new users and those maintaining long-term streaks.
